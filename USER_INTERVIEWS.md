# User Interviews

## Objective

The goal of early user conversations was to better understand:
- AI tooling pain points
- subscription management challenges
- operational AI spending concerns

---

# Interviewed Personas

## Startup Founder
Pain Points:
- rapidly increasing AI expenses
- unclear ROI across tools
- overlapping subscriptions

---

## Engineering Student
Pain Points:
- paying for multiple AI coding assistants
- uncertainty around best-value tools

---

## Small Team Operator
Pain Points:
- managing multiple AI vendors
- lack of visibility into overall AI spending

---

# Key Insights

## AI Tool Sprawl
Many users subscribe to:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Cursor
- Copilot

simultaneously without clear optimization.

---

## Limited Cost Visibility
Users often:
- underestimate total AI spend
- forget inactive subscriptions
- lack centralized reporting

---

## Executive Reporting Need
Users found:
- savings summaries
- optimization recommendations
- shareable reports

particularly valuable.

---

# Product Validation

The interviews validated demand for:
- centralized AI spend visibility
- actionable optimization recommendations
- lightweight executive reporting
