# Product Metrics

## Overview

StackSave focuses on measuring both:
- product engagement
- cost optimization impact

---

# Core Product Metrics

## Audit Completion Rate
Measures:
- percentage of users who complete audits

Importance:
- validates onboarding quality
- indicates UX effectiveness

---

# Savings Metrics

## Average Monthly Savings Identified
Measures:
- average monthly optimization opportunities per audit

Importance:
- core product value indicator

---

## Total Savings Identified
Measures:
- aggregate savings generated across all audits

Importance:
- demonstrates platform impact

---

# Engagement Metrics

## Report Share Rate
Measures:
- percentage of reports shared publicly

Importance:
- indicates perceived report value

---

## PDF Export Rate
Measures:
- percentage of reports exported as PDFs

Importance:
- validates executive reporting usefulness

---

# Retention Metrics

## Repeat Audit Rate
Measures:
- users returning for multiple audits

Importance:
- indicates recurring product value

---
